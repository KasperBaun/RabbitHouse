# OpenSCAD rabbit house

This repository contains OpenSCAD code for a rabbit house.  
The design includes a main house structure and an attached run for the rabbits to exercise.  

## Requirements
- The rabbits must have a cool place to hide should the weather be hot.
- The rabbits must have a dry place to hide should the weather be rainy.
- The rabbits must have a place to exercise and play. It should be possible for them to do binkies and run at full speed.
- The rabbits must be protected from predators, such as cats and birds of prey.
- The rabbits must be protected from the wind, especially in the winter.
- The rabbits must be protected from the sun, especially in the summer.
- The rabbits must have access to fresh air and natural light.
- The rabbits must have access to food and water.
- The rabbits must have access to a litter box or a designated area for waste.
